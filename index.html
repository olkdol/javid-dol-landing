Position Sizing, and Why We Recommend 1,000+ USDT
📐

Why 1,000 USDT Minimum

Each entry sizes at up to 1/300th of your account balance per trade — that's the mechanism that spreads risk across dozens of simultaneous positions instead of betting big on any single one. Below roughly 1,000 USDT, that per-trade slice gets small enough that exchange minimum-notional limits and fees start cutting into the edge. 1,000 USDT and up is where the strategy runs the way it was designed to.

FAQ
The Questions You'd Ask Before Paying

…except there's nothing to pay. It's free. Still, here's what you'll want to know:

Is it really free? No hidden costs?
Yes — no subscription, no purchase price, no performance fee. The only revenue source is Binance's official Broker Program: a share of the trading fee you already pay Binance gets rebated to us, by Binance. Your fees are exactly the same whether you sign up through our link or not.
Is it safe to enter my API key?
Your API key is encrypted and stored locally on your own PC — it is never transmitted anywhere. And most importantly: withdrawal permission is never required. An API key with withdrawals disabled cannot move your funds even if it leaked. Our guide explicitly tells you to leave withdrawal permission off.
Are profits guaranteed?
No. JaviD Future Bot is not a "guaranteed-profit bot" — it's a tool that executes a defined strategy 24/7 without emotion. Crypto futures carry a serious risk of loss, and every trading decision and outcome is your responsibility. That's exactly why we strongly recommend validating in Shadow Mode before going live.
How close is Shadow Mode to real trading?
Shadow Mode also uses the real exchange's live API, and trades run on virtual USDT — signals, timing, and market data are identical to live trading; only the real orders are withheld. It's the closest thing to trading with real money, at zero risk.
What happens to my positions if my PC shuts down mid-trade?
The bot runs on your PC, so if it powers off, new entries and position management stop. Positions already open remain on the exchange, and when you restart the program, the bot re-detects them and resumes managing them. This is why we recommend an always-on mini PC or a low-cost cloud VM.
What's the minimum capital?
We recommend 1,000 USDT or more. Each entry is sized at up to 1/300th of your balance to spread risk across many positions — below that level, exchange minimum-notional limits and fees start eating the strategy's edge. It will run on less, but 1,000+ USDT is where it works as designed.
What about flash crashes and liquidation risk?
Five layers of exit logic — liquidation-avoidance, ATR trailing stops, volume-spike detection and more — manage every position, but no system can fully eliminate the risk of violent moves in futures markets. Keep leverage conservative and only run capital you can afford to lose. We'd rather tell you this straight.
Can I use it without knowing how to code?
Yes. Download → double-click start.bat → paste your API key → pick coins (or one-click Top 20) → start. No code, anywhere. The User Manual walks through the entire flow in 9 real screens.
Which exchanges and markets are supported?
Currently Binance USDⓈ-M Futures only. The bot can monitor 500+ symbols simultaneously — pick your own list or use the "Top 20 by Turnover" preset.
Does it run on both Windows and macOS? What specs?
Yes — both Windows and macOS. No high-end hardware needed: any PC that stays on 24/7 with an internet connection is enough. If you don't want to keep your main PC running, the setup guide covers mini-PC and low-cost cloud VM options.
Do I have to sign up through your referral link?
You can use an existing Binance account. However, only accounts created through the partner link generate the fee rebate — and that rebate is the sole reason this software has no subscription fee. If you're creating a new account anyway, signing up through the link supports the project at zero extra cost to you.
How do I set up Telegram alerts?
Enter your Telegram bot token and chat ID in the Settings tab — that's it. You'll receive balance, cumulative P&L, ROI, win rate and open-position reports on request or on a schedule you define. The manual covers the setup with screenshots.
What's the refund policy?
The price is $0, so there's nothing to refund. Delete it anytime — no cancellation flow, no penalty. Start with Shadow Mode and see for yourself, risk-free.
How do updates work?
New versions are announced on this page and in the Telegram channel. Download and overwrite — your settings are preserved. Changelogs for major versions (V18, etc.) are posted alongside.
