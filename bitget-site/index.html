Running It For Real
Why we recommend starting with at least 1,000 USDT.

This isn't a minimum to make us money — JaviD Future Bot is free. It's a minimum for the strategies themselves to function the way they were designed to. Position sizing, stop distance, and per-symbol allocation are all calculated as a percentage of your balance. Fund the account too thin, and orders get rejected for falling below Bitget's minimum notional size before the strategy logic ever gets a fair shot. Starting at 1,000 USDT or more lets the position sizing engine actually do its job — real entries, real order flow, real results.

FAQ
Questions people ask before they download.

Is this a signal group or an actual bot?

An actual automated execution program. It reads the market, decides, and places/manages orders directly on your Bitget account — nobody is sending you manual calls to act on.

Do you ever have access to my funds?

No. The API key you connect can be restricted to trading-only, with withdrawal permission off. Even so, all execution happens locally on your PC — JaviD never holds, routes, or touches your balance.

What does it cost?

Nothing. It's free forever. We earn a small referral share from Bitget on the trading fees you'd already be paying — never a fee on top of your account.

Can I lose money?

Yes — this is real futures trading with real market risk. Shadow Mode lets you see how the strategies actually behave before you commit real capital, and you control position sizing and which coins are active at all times.

Do I need coding experience?

No. Strategies, coin selection, and risk settings are all configured through the dashboard — no scripting required.

Windows or macOS?

Both are supported and downloadable free once you have an access code.

How do I get an access code?

Join the Telegram community and sign up for Bitget through our official partner link — codes are issued free from there.

What's the minimum balance to start?

We recommend at least 1,000 USDT so position sizing and stop distances can function as designed — see "Running It For Real" above.

Can I run it on multiple coins at once?

Yes, up to 500+ symbols are monitored and you choose which are active from the dashboard.

Does it run while my PC is off?

No — execution happens on your own machine, so the PC needs to stay on and connected for the bot to keep trading.

Is my API key safe?

Keys are encrypted locally and never transmitted to any JaviD-owned server — the only outbound connection during trading is directly to Bitget.

What if a strategy keeps losing on a particular coin?

Disable it. Shadow Mode's trade history shows performance per symbol — uncheck any coin that isn't performing and save your selection.

Is there support if I get stuck?

Yes — the Telegram community and Community Board are both monitored for setup questions.
